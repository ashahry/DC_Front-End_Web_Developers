Membership

Members of the group come from various backgrounds. To join the group you must be passionate about front end technologies and be able to attend at least one session every month.

How to sign up

  • Contact us today so that we register your name.
  • After registeration, you will recieve a membership card in your email which you may print and bring with yourself.
  • Look for the upcoming sessions and attend. It's that simple!
  • Present your recent discoveries so that others benefit from your knowledge.

Presentation

We encourage each member to give a presentation of their work once every month.Your presentation can cover a broad range of web related topics.
