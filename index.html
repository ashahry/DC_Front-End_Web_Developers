About Us:

Founded in 2016, DC Front-End Web Developers consist of professionals from the nations's capital who get together weekly to share ideas and learn from one another. The group is open to everyone who is interested in front-end web development technologies. You are welcome to join us today!

During our weekly meetings:

  • Connect with the best minds in the industry to share ideas and brainstorm new solutions to challenging problems.
  • Hear industry leaders talk about what worked (and what didn't) so that you can save time on your most challenging projects.
  • Learn about the latest research and technologies that you can use immediately to invent the future.
