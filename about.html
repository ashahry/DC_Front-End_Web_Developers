About Us

Founded in 2016, DC Front-End Web Developers consist of web professionals from the nations's capital who get together weekly to share ideas and learn from one another. The group is open to everyone who is interested in front-end web development technologies. You are welcome to join us today!

You may find out how to become a member here.

During our Weekly Sessions

  • Connect with the best minds in the industry to share ideas and brainstorm new solutions to challenging problems.
  • Hear industry leaders talk about what worked (and what didn't) so that you can save time on your most challenging projects.
  • Learn about the latest research and technologies that you can use immediately to invent the future.

You may find out about our upcoming events here.

Present your ideas

Almost every week, we have some people who volunteer to present their recent discoveries and ideas they faced during their personal projects. We then ask questions and hear their answers. If you are interested to present your project please contact us so that we schedule your presentation.
